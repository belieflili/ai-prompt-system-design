# NZ Job Application Prompts
A collection of optimized prompts for New Zealand job searching, CV writing, and interview preparation.

[Paste JD here]

- Key skills required
- Soft vs hard skills
- What the candidate should highlight
- Possible interview questions
- Risk areas for the candidate
